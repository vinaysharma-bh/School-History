Viswashanti School History

A journey of educational excellence since 1993

1993

Founding

Viswasanthi English Medium High School was established in 1993 by M.P. Venkata Rao with the support of Bhattiprolu residents and like-minded friends. Their vision was to provide quality education and foster a love for learning in the rural community.
